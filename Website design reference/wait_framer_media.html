Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about the Wait template. Find answers to the most common questions below.

What is Wait?

Wait is a premium Framer template designed to help you create and manage a waitlist for your product launch. It enables you to grow your mailing list and build anticipation for your upcoming release.

What's included in this template?

The Wait template includes over 15 components, multiple code overrides, a thank you page, and a 404 page. These elements combine to help you create an engaging and effective waitlist site.

How do I customize this template?

Customizing the Wait template is straightforward with Framer's user-friendly interface. You can modify the components, implement code overrides, and adjust the design to align with your brand's look and functionality needs.

Is there support available?

Yes, for support, please reach out to the template author via email or on X @justinmfarrugia.

How much will this cost?

Wait will be free, forever.
